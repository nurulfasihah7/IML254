About myself, 7 July 2023

I have low self-esteem which is lack of self-confidence with my physical body. Even so, I am always surrounded by positive friends who help me from overthinking negatively about myself. LIGHT OF ELOQUENT

At 4 o'clock in the morning, on the 7th of July 2003, my mother risked her life just to bring me into this mortal world. My parents chose Hospital Seberang Jaya, Pulau Pinang just for me to be born safely. My beloved parents gave me the name Nurul Fasihah which means light of eloquent. Before I was born, my mother gave birth to 2 children, namely my eldest sister and my only brother, Nurul Fadzliyana and Muhammad Firdaus in 2 December 1994 and 3 October 1996. I am the youngest and last child in my small family. We live in our small flat house in Seberang Jaya, Pulau Pinang. I have various nicknames given by people around me. In school, the teacher calls me Fasihah while some of my friends call me Fas, Peah, Sihah, and even Cihah, but at home the nicknames from my family members is quite special in my heart, which is Eah. I'm a spoiled child with my family members but I'll be very awkward with strangers. Sometimes I like to tease my friends because I am also a cheerful, hilarious and happy go lucky person. I can be categorized as an ambivert which means introvert with outsiders but extrovert with people close to me.
