My Collections with My Friend, Fatini Mashitah.

Fatini and I loved to collect and hunting limited books together. She likes English books more than Malay books unlike me. I love Malay books more than English books. I will keep my other books collection at Fatini's house as my father didn't fully support my hobby but he allows me to keep only half of my books at our house so the others will be kept at Fatini's house. Sometimes, we will exchange our books. She will borrow my Malay books while I will borrow her English books as well. We will share our opinions about those books we read with each other. I felt happy as I have a friend who shares the same hobby with me.

Books gives me some kind of healing that no one can for me.

I always find books can treat me from the hard reality that I have to face. Sometimes fictional characters in these books can calm my inner soul.

These are my collections of books at my small library home.



These are my 2 limited edition novels from author, Elvroseth. She collabs with Ink Loaf Publication to make a Prince of Death hardcover with sprayed edges. Besides Prince of Death hardcover is the notebook of Hijaiyah that was also produced by Ink Loaf Publication.

These are my favourite collections from Luce Del Lio. She produced these novels by her own. I know her from Instagram as she keeps promoting her books. I got attracted by the cover of the book as it was black which is my favourite colour.

Sometimes I find that it is really tiring to have this kind of hobby because I often don't have much money to buy the books I want so I have to be frugal with the money I get all this time just to maintain this hobby. As for me, my book reading phase is getting less and less because I'm always too busy to read them so I can only keep them as my collection on my small shelf. I started from reading regular books to becoming a collector of limited edition books now.

Besides that, my parents don't really support my hobby because for them it's just a waste of money and time but I never felt angry for my parents because what they advised was also right but for me to be able to see my books in my little shelf gave me infinite satisfaction. However, they didn't really throw away my book. They just advised me to spend less on books and prioritize other important needs. They also allowed me to keep some part of my collection at home and the rest of it at Fatini's house. While my friends always give me books as presents that I like and can't buy for some reason on my birthday every year. I'm grateful to be surrounded by good friends who always support my hobby even though my parents didn't.
